About Us

Where knowledge meets compassion in caregiver training

Our Mission

At Heal and Pure, our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge, skills, and compassion needed to provide exceptional care to those in need. We believe that quality caregiving begins with quality training.

Our History

Founded in 2024 by healthcare professionals with 20+ years of experience, Heal and Pure was born from a vision to elevate caregiving standards in Nepal. What started small is now a CTEVT-recognized leader in caregiver education.

Our Approach

We combine classroom instruction with real-world practical training. Students graduate with not only technical knowledge, but empathy, emotional intelligence, and confidence to serve with dignity.

Our Facilities

  • Modern classrooms with AV equipment
  • Practical simulation labs
  • Resource library & study spaces
  • Comfortable student lounge area
