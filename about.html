About Heal and Pure

Heal and Pure is a modern, CTEVT-certified caregiver and health training center in Pokhara, Nepal. Our mission is to empower individuals with practical skills and confidence for a rewarding career in healthcare, elderly care, and wellness.

2018: Founded

Heal and Pure opens its doors in Pokhara, focusing on CTEVT-certified caregiver training.

2020: International Expansion

Placement support for students in Japan, Australia, and Europe begins.

2023: 3000+ Graduates

Celebrating thousands of successful students and a 95% satisfaction rate.
